About Julianna

Originally from Orange County, CA, Julianna Conley is a MSJ candidate at Northwestern's Medill School of Journalism, specializing in magazine writing and concentrating in data journalism. She graduated cum laude from the University of Notre Dame's honors program with majors in Sociology and Pre-Health Studies and a minor in Journalism, Ethics, and Democracy. After completing her undergraduate studies, she earned her Master's of Education while teaching through AmeriCorps.

Julianna’s work focuses on education, community and human-centered features. Most recently, she’s covered Chicago’s Bronzeville and Bridgeport neighborhoods as an education beat reporter. A versatile writer, she’s also written a consistently trending column for her college newspaper, creative nonfiction for an academic journal, PR articles and copy for newsletters. In addition to print journalism, Julianna’s developed multiple podcasts, two of which won "Best Podcast" and "3rd Best Podcast" from the Indiana Collegiate Press Awards.
